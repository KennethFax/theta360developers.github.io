Who are these bozos?
Jesse Casman and Craig Oda went to a TechCrunch Disrupt SF Hackathon in 2015 and built an initial Unofficial API Guide to help out our friends at who were launching the amazing THETA S 360 camera. We're not professional developers and we don't work for RICOH. We work at Oppkey, a developers relations firm in San Francisco. We do consult for RICOH from time to time, but this is not a RICOH site and is not authorized by RICOH. It's a community site by developers for developers. If there's a problem, blame us, not them. Expect problems. For the real information, please use the official RICOH documentation. Please tell us what would be useful for you.
